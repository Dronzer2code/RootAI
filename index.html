How Root AI Works for You

Our simple four-step process helps you turn data into decisive action for a thriving farm.

We gather crucial information from your fields using satellites, drones, ground sensors, and your farm records.

2. AI Analyzes

Our powerful AI algorithms, trained on Indian agricultural data, process and interpret this information.

3. Actionable Insights

You receive clear, easy-to-understand recommendations directly on your smartphone or computer dashboard.

4. Farm Smarter
